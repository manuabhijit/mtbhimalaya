Discounts

+ There is a 20% discount for students and bonafide Himachal Pradesh residents.

+ Please note that supporters for riders will also follow the same fee structure.

+ The organizers reserve the right to award waiver of any kind on the registration fees to anyone.

+ Only Individual Solo riders and Teams of two (2) participants qualify for participation.

+ This pricing is applicable for all the registration & confirmation on or before 5 PM IST ,10th September,2017 .

+ 10% discount to past MTB Himalaya riders.
Note: Payments can also be made via PayPal

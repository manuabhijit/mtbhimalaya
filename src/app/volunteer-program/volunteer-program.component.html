Welcome to the Volunteer Program

Work behind the scenes of Asia’s oldest mountain bike race- 13th Hero MTB Himalaya 2017!

The dynamics of a live mountain biking stage race require super talented individuals who can fit in multiple roles. Logistical challenges are a big task to handle when it comes to management. This calls for people who can flex themselves on short timelines, little sleep, untimed meals, lots of travel and huge learning opportunities!
Two weeks spent working with our teams round the clock (no kidding about this part) and travelling from Erstwhile British Captial- Shimla to Little Lhasa- Dharamshala will probably be the most stressful and yet amazingly wonderful events you shall cherish in years to come!

Social Media and Communication

One of the most important jobs at the race- The guys who control what the world sees. Get an opportunity to feed the eyeballs of >1million audiences, Churn quirky and crisp captions within unrealistic timelines, Interact with the participants and sponsors at the race.

If slow internet, power banks and constant system crashes do not creep you out, you are the best fit for this job!

Key traits- Splendid communication skills, ornate writing ability and willingness to work long hours.

Print and Digital media management

Are you good at managing people? Do you have a journalism degree? Do newsroom stories excite you? Can you build contacts?

We are looking for people who can manage writers, journalists and reporters. If you have any experience in media and it's management- this is for you.

Photographers

Don't expect a comfortable ride, a complete meal and a sharp hairstyle with this profile. You are going to be the one constantly on the move and the first ones to cover and experience all the action - LIVE!

It will be through your lens the world will be seeing the event. Get an opportunity to work along some of the best professionals in the field and experience the best MTB adventure.

Videographers + Editors (Incentives and long term opportunities attached)

Offering you a very exclusive opportunity to work on appealing visual content. Adventure sport videos are the next big thing on the block, taking away social media by a storm.

You are going to be a part of a very special team that covers the action behind Hero MTB Himalaya. We are looking for people who can shoot and edit to churn out engaging videos that catch attention. Special incentives and opportunity to work long term available based on performance. Applicants must carry their own device for editing.

Eligibility Criteria

In-order to be eligible for applying to our Volunteer program

Eligibility Criteria:
  1. You must be between 18-28 years of age with no medical condition.
  2. You must also be able to stay for the duration of your assigned schedule.
  3. We are looking for candidates with good communication skills, (knowledge of a third language is a plus)
  4. You must be an adventure seeker with unconditional love for nature.
Your entitlements would be:
  1. Proper accommodation on campsites
  2. Transportation between stages
  3. Transportation of luggage between stages
  4. All meals on race days
  5. Exclusive Volunteer race merchandise
  6. Letter of appreciation and race memorabilia
Things not included:
  1. Travel to Shimla and from Dharamshala
  2. Any additional expenses incurred during your stay
Note: For further questions, you can email: Kshitiz Gupta, Chief Volunteer Monk on kshitiz.gupta@hastpa.org

Apply only if you are available from 27th Sept to 7th Oct
